Our Mission & Actions

Sustainable development and immediate care for the people of Madagascar.

Education & Youth

We believe education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. F3MS provides school kits, uniforms, and supports the renovation of classrooms to ensure every child can learn in a safe and supportive environment.

EDUCATION PHOTO

Healthcare Access

Our association facilitates access to essential medical services. We work with local clinics to provide consultations, medicines, and emergency medical support for families who cannot afford treatment.

HEALTH PHOTO

Food Security

Fighting malnutrition is at the heart of our daily actions. Through regular distributions and community gardens, we ensure that the most vulnerable households have access to nutritious food.
